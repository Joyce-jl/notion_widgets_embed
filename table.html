Appearance of the NPC's Map Icon versus a regular merchant icon

Pact Supply Network Agents are special karma merchants that are made available by training the third level of the Pact Commander mastery, Supply Line Management. Unlocking Tyrian masteries requires a Guild Wars 2 expansion. There are six merchants in total, one per region of Tyria. Each of them moves to a different location every day. You may only purchase one item from each vendor per day.

You can reach this page from in-game at any time by typing /wiki psna into the chat box.
